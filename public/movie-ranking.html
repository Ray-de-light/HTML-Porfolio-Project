Ray's Recommendations of the best Movies

To See Before Tuesday

Sharshank Redemption

(US) I've watched this so many times and still love it!

Wild Tales

(Argentina) a collection of 6 short stories about getting revenge when life's frustrations reach absurd levels.

Groundhog Day

(US) An excellent film based on the perineal idea: What if I could live my life over again.

Run, Lola, Run

(Germany) Another take on the previous idea, but completely different.

Good Will Hunting

(US) We all want to be that smart. Great performances, great concept.

There's Something About Mary

(US) One of the top, absurd comedies of all time.

Casablanca

(US) See it!

The Man Without a Past

(Finland) What would happen if you suddenly, and permanently, lost your memory far from home? Where would your life go from there?
